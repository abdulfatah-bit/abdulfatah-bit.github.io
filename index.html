About Me

I am a Counseling Psychologist and researcher with a Master’s degree in Counseling Psychology from the University of Iringa. With over five years of experience in public health and mental health research, I focus on healthcare integration, stigma reduction, and improving mental health support systems. I have published academic papers and secured competitive scholarships, including the Turkish Government Scholarship. Fluent in English, Turkish, and Swahili.

Research Interests

  • Mental health and public health integration
  • Healthcare access and equity
  • Psychopathologies and psychosocial support systems
  • Stigma reduction
  • Cultural and social factors in health-seeking behavior
